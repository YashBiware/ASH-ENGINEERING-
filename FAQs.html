Get answers to common questions about our pesticide manufacturing machinery and services

What types of machines do you manufacture for the pesticide industry?

We provide a complete range of machinery including bottle filling machines (automatic & semi-automatic), cap sealing machines, self-adhesive labeling machines, ribbon blenders, basket extruders, tray dryers, chemical mixing vessels, vibro sifters, and more.

Can your machines handle different pesticide formulations (liquid, powder, granules)?

Yes, our machines are designed to handle various pesticide formulations. For example, ribbon blenders and mixers are ideal for powders, while filling machines are used for liquid pesticides.

Do you offer customization of machines based on client requirements?

Absolutely. We customize capacity, automation level, material (SS/MS), and safety features according to your production needs.

Are your machines suitable for small-scale as well as large-scale pesticide production?

Yes, we supply both semi-automatic machines for small/medium manufacturers and fully automatic systems for large-scale production lines.

What safety measures are included in your machines for handling chemicals?

Our machines are built with corrosion-resistant materials, leak-proof systems, explosion-proof motors, and operator-friendly designs to ensure maximum safety during pesticide manufacturing.

Do you provide installation and training after purchase?

Yes, we provide on-site installation, setup, and operator training to ensure smooth and safe operation of all our machines.

What is the maintenance requirement of your machines?

Most machines require only basic routine checks, lubrication, and cleaning. We also provide AMC (Annual Maintenance Contracts) for long-term support.

Can your filling and sealing machines handle different bottle sizes?

Yes, our filling and sealing systems are adjustable to accommodate a variety of bottle shapes and sizes.

Do you export machines outside India?

Yes, we serve both domestic and international clients and can handle packaging, documentation, and shipping.
